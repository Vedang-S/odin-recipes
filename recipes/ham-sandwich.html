DELICIOUS HAM SANDWICH RECIPE

Description

This simple ham sandwich is made with your choice of bread, topped with deli ham, and complemented by cheese and condiments. Add lettuce and tomato for extra freshness, then enjoy a quick and easy meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (any type you prefer)
  • 2–3 slices of deli ham
  • 1 slice of cheese (optional, e.g., Swiss or cheddar)
  • Condiments (e.g., mayonnaise, mustard, or butter)
  • Lettuce (optional)
  • Tomato slices (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare Bread: Lay two slices of bread on a flat surface.
  2. Spread Condiments: Spread your choice of condiments (mayonnaise, mustard, or butter) on one or both slices of bread.
  3. Layer Ham: Place 2–3 slices of deli ham on one slice of bread.
  4. Add Cheese and Veggies (Optional): If desired, add a slice of cheese, lettuce, and tomato.
  5. Assemble Sandwich: Place the second slice of bread on top to close the sandwich.
  6. Serve: Cut the sandwich into halves or quarters if desired, and serve immediately. Enjoy!
