DELICIOUS LASAGNA RECIPE

Description

This simple lasagna recipe layers cooked noodles, a savory meat sauce, and a creamy ricotta filling, topped with melted mozzarella. It’s easy to assemble, bake, and perfect for a hearty, comforting meal that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef or sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (divided)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook Lasagna Noodles: Boil 9 lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare Meat Sauce: In a large skillet, brown 1 lb ground beef or sausage over medium heat. Add 1 chopped onion and 2 minced garlic cloves, cooking until softened.
  4. Add Sauce and Seasonings: Stir in 24 oz of marinara sauce, 1 teaspoon dried basil, and 1 teaspoon dried oregano. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Mix Ricotta Filling: In a mixing bowl, combine 15 oz ricotta cheese, 1 egg, and 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Assemble Lasagna: Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer 3 lasagna noodles, then half of the ricotta mixture, followed by a third of the remaining meat sauce, and some shredded mozzarella.
  7. Repeat Layers: Add another layer of 3 noodles, the rest of the ricotta mixture, another third of the meat sauce, and some mozzarella. Top with the final 3 noodles, the remaining meat sauce, and the rest of the shredded mozzarella.
  8. Bake: Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Cool and Serve: Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
