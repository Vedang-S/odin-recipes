DELICIOUS RAMEN RECIPE

Description

This simple ramen recipe features tender noodles in a savory broth made with soy sauce, miso (optional), and sesame oil. Topped with a soft-boiled egg, fresh green onions, and your choice of vegetables, it’s a quick and satisfying meal that’s easy to customize. Perfect for a cozy, homemade bowl of comfort!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 packet instant ramen seasoning (or 1 tablespoon soy sauce)
  • 1 teaspoon miso paste (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 package ramen noodles
  • 1 egg
  • Green onions (for garnish)
  • Vegetables (e.g., spinach, corn, bok choy)
  • Optional toppings: chili flakes, nori (seaweed), sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Boil water: Bring 4 cups of water to a boil in a medium pot.
  2. Season broth: Add the instant ramen seasoning (or soy sauce), miso paste (optional), and sesame oil to the boiling water. Stir to dissolve the seasoning.
  3. Cook noodles: Add the ramen noodles to the pot and cook according to the package instructions, typically 3-4 minutes.
  4. Prepare egg: While the noodles cook, soft-boil the egg by simmering it in a separate pot of boiling water for 7 minutes. Then, peel the egg.
  5. Prepare toppings: Slice the green onions and chop any vegetables you'd like to add, such as spinach, corn, or bok choy.
  6. Assemble ramen: Once the noodles are cooked, pour the broth and noodles into a bowl.
  7. Add toppings: Top the ramen with the boiled egg, green onions, and vegetables. Optional: add chili flakes, nori, or sesame seeds for extra flavor.
  8. Serve: Enjoy your simple ramen hot!
